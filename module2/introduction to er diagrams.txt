Entity-Relationship models:-

puropse:high level picture of the database

Entity:object that can be uniquely identified
eg:customer,product

Attributes:properties/features of an entity or relationships (represented by ellipse)

key attribute:uniquely identified attribute(represented by ellipse with underline)

multi-valued attibutes:an attribute containing multiple values(represented by multi-stroke ellipse)

derived attribute:which can be derived from other attributes(represented by dotted ellipse)

compound attributes:extracted from main attribute

relationships:related two or more tables(represented by diamond)

total and partial participation:entities participating in a relation.
= two lines represents total participation
- one line represents partial participation
